Ugali Samaki

Ugali is a staple in almost every Kenyan home. The best thing about ugali is that you can pair it with any soup or vegetable and make a good meal out of it.

Below is a recipe for crispy Tilapia on creamy spinach and terere with ugali and grilled matoke.

Ingredients

  • 100 Grams Maize/Corn Flour
  • 200b Grams Water
  • 20 ML Salted Butter
  • 1 PC Medium Size Lemon
  • Salt & Crashed Pepper to taste
  • 1 LT Corn Oil
  • 150 Grams Terere (Half Cooked)
  • 50 Grams Onion Chopped

Step by Step Directions

  1. Boil water then add half of the maize flour and mix it with a wooden spoon continuously
  2. Reduce the heat and add the remaining flour.
  3. Cook it for 15-20mins and then add butter and mix well.
  4. Remove the ugali from fire and mold it in a round ring before it sets
  5. Fry the onions with oil until soft and transarent, add terere and mix well then season with salt and pepper and remove from the fire.
  6. Arrange the terere on the ugali while still hot.
  7. Marinate the fish with salt, pepper and lemon then grill it till golden brown, but not crispy.
  8. Place on the terere and serve all hot.
  9. Garnish the plate with slices of mangoes and grilled matoke.
